Around ten days before each upcoming event, we will release a sign-up form on our Facebook page. Be sure to like us to get the quickest notifications, as only a maximum of 25 participants are permitted per event. Admission ranges from $6 - $11 depending on the cost of ingredients. Interested students, however, are encouraged to purchase an annual membership for $10 and pay lower admission fees at each of our events. You can obtain a membership on Imagine Day, Clubs Day, as well as during any of our events.
