Teriyaki

Cooking Club’s first workshop of 2018/19! Teriyaki is a highly versatile sauce found frequently in Japanese cooking. It can be used for many different dishes! Learn how to make a simple, yet delicious teriyaki sauce and then use it to make teriyaki chicken or teriyaki anything!

September 24th, 2018

Spaghetti & Meatballs

Our second event of the year, Spaghetti and Meatballs, is a popular Americanized spaghetti dish that originated from Italian immigrants in the USA during the 1880s to 1920s. Come and learn how to make homemade meatballs and a quick, simple tomato sauce from scratch!

October 10th, 2018

Poutine

There is one dish that no restaurants in Vancouver have perfected in the traditional manner. We present to you: Poutine! Learn how to make “Poutine Sauce” not “Gravy”, and how to properly prepare fries. This won't be just any poutine, but the best you’ll ever make!

October 22nd, 2018

Fettucine Alfredo

Why didn’t the fettuccine go out for dinner? It was too alfredo! Don’t be too alfredo make this popular pasta dish! Come learn how to make this simple but tasty creamy pasta!

November 5th, 2018

Bibimbap

Fun, colourful, delicious and nutritious - come cook a popular Korean dish with us - Bibimbap!

November 19th, 2018

Shrimp Spring Rolls

Been working on that summer bod all summer? No? I didn’t think so either! Turn those summer rolls into spring rolls; just add a bit of seasoning! Reminisce summer by learning how to make fresh Vietnamese spring rolls!

December 3rd, 2018
