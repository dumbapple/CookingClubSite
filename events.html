Uramaki

Uramaki, or the inside out roll is any kind of sushi roll in which the rice of the sushi has been inverted to the outside. It’s creation is credited to Hidekazu Tojo, who allegedly created the first California Rolls here in Vancouver in the late 1970’s. Since then Uramaki has become a staple in restaurants across the world and a way for chefs to bring creativity to the cuisine of sushi.
