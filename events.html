Time and place (unless otherwise specified): 6:00PM - 8:00PM @ Irving K Barber Learning Centre (IBLC) - 155
Teriyaki

Cooking Club’s first workshop of 2018/19! Teriyaki is a highly versatile sauce found frequently in Japanese cooking. It can be used for many different dishes! Learn how to make a simple, yet delicious teriyaki sauce and then use it to make teriyaki chicken or teriyaki anything!

September 24th, 2018

Mabo Tofu

Our second event of the year, Mabo Tofu, is a popular dish originating from Sichuan, China. Come and learn how to make the Japanese variation. It’s salty, sweet, and slightly spicy all at the same time! It’s like a party on your tongue.
