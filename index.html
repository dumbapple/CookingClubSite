About Us

Mission

Our goal is to create a welcoming environment where students can learn how to cook delicious meals. We want to share our passion for cooking by providing guidance for anyone wishing to explore their inner chef.

Taste new flavours

Our creations will vary in style at each event, so get ready to open your taste buds!

We're here to help

Events are open to people of all skill levels. Clear instructions will be given, and assistance from the cooking team will always be available.

Cooking Events

Roughly 25 participants may be accommodated per event. We will open a registration form approximately ten days before each scheduled event, so be sure to like our Facebook page to be notified as early as possible.

Admission ranges from $6 - $11 depending on the cost of ingredients. Interested students, however, can purchase an annual membership for $10 and pay lower admission fees. Members also get to register for events one day prior to general participants.

Memberships can be obtained on Imagine Day and Clubs Day. They may also be purchased at any of our events. We look forward to seeing you!
