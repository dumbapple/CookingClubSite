About Us

Mission

Our goal has always been to create a welcoming environment where students can learn how to cook tasty foods. We share our passion for food by providing guidance for anyone seeking to explore their inner chef.

Taste new flavours

Our creations will vary in style at each event, so get ready to open your tastebuds!

We're here to help

Events are open to people of all skill levels. We lay out all the steps needed to create these dishes, and are always willing to provide more assistance.

Cooking Events

A maximum of 25 participants is allowed per event. We will open a registration form approximately ten days before the scheduled event, so be sure to like our Facebook page to be notified as early as possible.

Admission ranges from $6 - $11 depending on the cost of ingredients. Interested students, however, can purchase an annual membership for $10 and pay lower admission fees. Memberships can be obtained on Imagine Day, Clubs Day, as well as during any of our events. We look forward to seeing you!
